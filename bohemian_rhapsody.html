About the Song

"Bohemian Rhapsody" is a song by the British rock band Queen, released in 1975. It is known for its unique structure, blending elements of rock, ballad, and opera. The song has become one of Queen's most iconic tracks and is celebrated for its innovative production and powerful vocals.
